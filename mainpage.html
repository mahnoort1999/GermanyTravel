Germany

Germany is one of Europe's most fascinating destinations. It is located in Central Europe,

is bordered by Poland, Czech Reublic, Austria, Switzerland, France, Luxembourg, Belgium,

The Netherlands, and Denmark. The cities are incredible. The food is great. Diversity exists.

It is an interesting place to visit if you like to travel, learn about different cultures,

religion, food, etc.
